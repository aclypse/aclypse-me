About

I was born in Khmelnytskyi, Ukraine. From what I can remember, a paper with a pencil have always been in my hand. Ever since I was little I knew that art will always be part of my life. Before I found the magical world of design I thought my true vocation is to become an illustrator. At this point of my life I'm a college student specializing in art and applied design, but my passion stands for graphic and digital design. Aside from all the artistic stuff I also have hobbies in different spheres (and passion for coffee). My main goal is to present unusual and unique solutions for this world.
